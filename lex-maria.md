# Lex Maria

- [[go]] https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lex_Maria
  - A [[swedish]] [[law]] that requires care takers to report events that *could* have caused injury or harm.
- [[olofl]] asks: should we have something like this for society level harm? E.g. projects that don't make sense and don't go anywhere, but still manage to consume huge amounts of resources that could be put to better use.

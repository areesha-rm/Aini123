# public utility

- This [[agora]] tries to run and support projects of [[public utility]]. These are some of the [[goals]] tracked in this [[agora]] as of the time of writing.
  - To contribute to the well being of the [[open source]] community and the [[federation]] through the [[fedstoa]].
    - To implement [[bridges]] and [[siphons]], like those hosted in [[moa]], which give users back control over their data and otherwise try to fulfill constructive declarative [[intents]].
    - To implement [[agora rfcs]] that promote the interoperation of tools and systems in the [[personal knowledge management]] space.
  - To contribute to [[effective altruism]] and other projects and movements in the space of incremental [[world improvement]], in particular as enabled by digital tools and platforms.
    - To support [[universal basic income]] experiments.
    - To promote [[liquid democracy]].
    - To explore [[protopoi]].

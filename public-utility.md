# public utility

- This [[agora]] tries to run projects of [[public utility]]. These are some of them as of the time of writing.
  - To contribute to the well being of the [[open source]] [[federation]] through the [[fedstoa]].
    - To implement [[bridges]] and [[siphons]], for example hosted in [[moa]], which give users back control over their data and otherwise try to fulfill constructive declarative [[intents]].
    - To implement [[agora rfcs]] to promote the interoperation of tools in the [[personal knowledge management]] space.
  - To contribute to [[effective altruism]] and other projects in the space of [[world improvement]], in particular as enabled by digital tools and platforms.
    - To support online [[universal basic income]] experiments.
    - To explore [[protopoi]] space.

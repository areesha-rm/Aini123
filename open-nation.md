# Open Nation

- An [[open nation]] is any nation organized around compassionate [[open ethics]].
  - Most [[open nations]] have [[free speech]].
  - Many [[open nations]] have [[open borders]].
  - Some [[open nations]] have [[universal basic income]].
  - [[Flancia]] is an open nation.
- [[Open nations]] reject [[hierarchy]] as a principle to organize [[society]].
